This functionality consists of a tutoring platform, with the main purpose of increasing social interaction between students and promoting an environment where students of the same faculty are encouraged to help each other.


The platform gives the possibility to students with pedagogical inclinations to offer punctual help to those who encounter difficulties in understanding certain subjects within the faculty by planning mentoring sessions.

Within the PoliMeets application, this functionality implements a form of student transparent feedback, being an improved alternative to the classic Feedback Forms on Moodle.


The collection of feedback within the platform aims to improve the teaching activities while also offering students the possibility to view and offer feedback for their upcoming subjects.
